Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an avenue for the community to participate in the performing arts; to develop the skills of our members and others in the theater arts (acting, directing, technical and administrative); to offer a variety of entertaining and cultural productions; and, to expand the number of people exposed to the arts through theatrical productions and activities.

Tickets at The Village Theater

Visit The Village Theater’s website and use the ‘performance by month‘ search to view and order tickets for Spotlight Player’s upcoming productions.

Spotlight Players is a 501 (c) 3 nonprofit, community theater company based in Canton. As you can imagine, it is increasingly difficult to support the cost of quality theater and day-to-day expenses on ticket sales alone. We depend on the generosity of individuals and sponsors to allow us to continue to perform in and for the community. Spotlight Players is eternally grateful to the companies in the community that generously offer fundraising opportunities to allow us to bond with one another and raise funds! We invite you to become participate in the many fundraising events we will be hosting this season! Visit our fundraiser page to learn more!
